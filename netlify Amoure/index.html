Our Mission

Dating apps broke something.

Endless swiping, toxic dynamics, and zero accountability turned dating into a game nobody wins. Amourè was built to fix that — with intentional matches, a respect-first culture, and features designed to surface your best self, not your most swipeable photo.

Why Amourè

Different by design.

We didn’t tweak a broken formula. We started over.

No infinite scroll

You are not a deck of cards.

So we stopped treating you like one. Every day you get a small, hand-matched set of people chosen for genuine compatibility — not a thousand faces to thumb past at 1am. Fewer choices, made with more care, so the ones you do see actually matter.

No ghost zone

Nobody gets left on read.

We’ve all been the one waiting on a reply that never comes. On Amourè, matches expire if no one engages, so conversations stay alive and intentional. Accountability isn’t a feature we bolted on — it’s the way the whole thing is built.

Verified, every one

Everyone here is real.

Catfish, bots, and recycled stock photos don’t make it past the door. Every profile is verified before it ever reaches you, because a real connection has to start with a real person. You should never have to wonder if who you’re talking to actually exists.

Manners Score

Kindness has a score.

Every interaction on Amourè shapes your Manners Score — how you respond, how you engage, how you treat people. Members with high scores rise in visibility. Those who disrespect others, fade.

Respect isn’t optional here. It’s infrastructure.

Community Guidelines

Last Updated: April 6, 2026

Amourè is a space built on dignity, respect, and genuine connection.

The golden rule: treat every person on this platform the way you want to be treated.

01

Be Authentic

Use real photos of yourself. Write an honest bio. Be who you say you are. Catfishing, impersonation, and fake profiles result in permanent bans.

02

Be Respectful

No means no. If someone does not respond or asks you to stop, respect that immediately. Persistent messaging after being ignored or rejected is harassment and will result in removal.

03

Be Safe

Never share your financial information with matches. Be cautious about sharing your home address or workplace. Meet new matches in public places first and tell someone where you are going.

04

No Ghosting

Always close connections gracefully. If it is not a match, say so kindly. Disappearing without a word is not acceptable on Amourè. Closing conversations with care is part of who we are.

05

Zero Tolerance Violations

The following result in immediate permanent removal with no warning:

  • Sending unsolicited explicit or sexual images
  • Any sexual content involving minors — this is reported to law enforcement
  • Threats, blackmail, or extortion
  • Sharing another user's private information (doxxing)
  • Sex trafficking, solicitation, or escort services
  • Racism, homophobia, transphobia, or hate speech of any kind
  • Creating multiple accounts after being banned
06
